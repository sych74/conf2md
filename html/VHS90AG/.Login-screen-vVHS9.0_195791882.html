Virtuozzo Hybrid Server 9.0 Admin Guide : .Login screen vVHS9.0

Created by Roksoliana Ben, last modified on May 02, 2024

To access your Control Panel, you must first provide a username and password.

Check the Remember me box to have the CP remember your login details for one month. You must enter your login credentials again after you log out or clear your browser cookies. 

After two failed attempts at logging in to the OnApp account, the system will show the number of login attempts that the user has left. After the login attempts limit is exhausted, the account will be locked, and the unlock instructions will be sent to the user's email. You can set the number of login attempts at Control Panel > Admin > Settings > Configuration > Defaults by configuring the Lockout attempts parameter.

If you have forgotten your password, follow the Forgot your password? link and specify the email to which your reset password instructions will be sent.

You may face issues with logging in to your Control Panel in the following scenarios:

  • If you enter invalid credentials that is your login and/or password.
  • If an IP address from which you try to access your CP is not added to a White List.

To successfully log in to CP, ensure that you enter valid credentials and that your IP is in the White List.

Ignore the options specified in your Control Panel but not mentioned in the documents.
