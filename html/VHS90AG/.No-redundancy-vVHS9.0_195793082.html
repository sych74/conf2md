The danger of data loss!

Without redundancy, singular chunks are stored on failure domains, one per failure domain. If the node or disk fails, the data may be lost. Having no redundancy is highly not recommended no matter the scenario, unless you only want to evaluate Virtuozzo Storage on a single server.
